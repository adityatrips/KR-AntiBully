The Three S's of Bullying

Share

If ever you are facing problems in your school, or university, the first step should be sharing what all is happening to a trusted person

Stop

You as a by-stander of a bullying situation should intervene and try to handle the situation by stopping the bully to your full extent

Spread

Lastly, but the most important step is to spread awareness about the bullying that is happening currently in our society
